Start From Any Level

At Fun Barista Academy, coffee is more than a drink. It is craft, service, and creativity. Whether you are a beginner or improving your technique, our courses guide your growth in a clear, step-by-step way.

What you will learn:
1. Espresso calibration and extraction basics
2. Latte art technique and milk texturing
3. Professional workflow and equipment handling
4. Customer service for real-world cafe shifts
